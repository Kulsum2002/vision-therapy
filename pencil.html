Pencil Push-up Exercise

  1. Hold a pencil at arm's length at the level of eyes and concentrate on the tip of the pencil.
  2. Slowly advance the tip towards your nose until you see a double image of the pencil.
  3. Hold the tip at that position and focus your eyes on it as long as possible, then relax.
  4. Repeat the above steps 10 times, morning and evening.
